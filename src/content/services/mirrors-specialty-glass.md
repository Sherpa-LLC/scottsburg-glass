## Custom Mirrors & Specialty Glass Solutions

Beyond windows and doors, Scottsburg Glass offers a comprehensive range of mirror and specialty glass services for both residential and commercial applications. From gym mirror walls to glass office partitions, our team delivers precision craftsmanship on every project.

- **Gym Mirror Walls** — Floor-to-ceiling safety-backed mirror installations for fitness centers, dance studios, yoga studios, and home gyms
- **Bathroom Mirrors** — Custom-cut vanity mirrors, medicine cabinet mirrors, and full-wall bathroom mirrors
- **Decorative Mirrors** — Statement mirrors for living rooms, entryways, and commercial lobbies
- **Commercial Mirrors** — Large-format mirrors for retail spaces, salons, and hospitality venues

- **Safety Glass** — Tempered and laminated glass for applications requiring enhanced protection
