## Our Story

Scottsburg Glass Inc. boasts over 40 years of expertise in the glass industry, serving Southern Indiana and beyond. We specialize in residential and commercial installations, ensuring every project reflects our commitment to quality and excellence. What started as a small operation has grown into the region's most trusted glass company, with over 4,000 windows installed and 1,500 doors completed.

## Our Values

We strive to lead the glass industry by providing exceptional craftsmanship and innovative solutions. Our team is equipped to handle a wide range of projects, from simple repairs to complex installations, ensuring every client receives the best service possible.

- **Professional** — We are quick, reliable, accommodating, and deeply knowledgeable about the glass trade
- **Quality Results** — Our goal is complete customer satisfaction with every job we undertake
- **Reliable Support** — We are here when you need us, for residential and commercial glass services alike
- **Continuous Training** — Our experienced and knowledgeable staff participate in continued hands-on training sessions

## Women's Business Enterprise

Scottsburg Glass Inc. is proudly certified as a Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) through 2026 by the State of Indiana. This certification reflects our commitment to excellence, diversity, and the highest standards of business practice.

## Our Mission

At Scottsburg Glass, we're just a call away from providing you with the glass services you need. Whether it's for residential or commercial purposes, we're here to assist with all your glass and glazing needs. Our knowledgeable team undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of industry standards, ensuring we deliver the most efficient and up-to-date service possible.

## Community Commitment

As a locally owned business rooted in Scottsburg, Indiana, we're more than just a glass company — we're your neighbors. We take pride in serving our community and building lasting relationships with every customer across Southern Indiana, from Scottsburg and Seymour to Columbus, Madison, and beyond.
